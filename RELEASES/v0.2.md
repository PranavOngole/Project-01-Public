## Context

v0.1 ships a working research-to-trade pipeline but with three critical gaps: no understanding of macro conditions (loses money in corrections), mechanical stops that don't defend thesis (sells good businesses on noise), and zero visibility into what the bot did or why. v0.2 closes all three: the engine now thinks in macro terms, defends theses when fundamentals break, and the owner can see every decision in real time.

## Outcomes

**Market regime awareness.** The bot now reads real Federal Reserve macro data and classifies markets as calm, elevated, or crisis. When regime shifts to elevated or crisis, the trading gate refuses new entries. This prevents the cascade of stops that drained v0.1 accounts in corrections.

**Thesis revalidation.** Instead of relying on intraday stop losses alone, the system runs a midday check: does the original thesis still hold? If fundamentals broke (earnings miss, CEO scandal), the system exits before full drawdown. Four output signals: hold, buy more, sell, or escalate to manual review.

**Permanent decision record.** Every trade decision (entry, exit, skip, and the reason) is now logged to a data warehouse with full context: conviction score, market regime, price at decision, and human-readable notes. The owner can audit the entire trading history and learn from what worked and what didn't.

**Owner dashboard.** The Cockpit terminal displays a live feed of recent decisions, current positions with real-time pricing, and the rationale behind each action. The owner goes from flying blind to full transparency.

**Automated discipline.** The regime gate is not discretionary—it's code. When macro conditions deteriorate, the system stops buying, period. No emotions, no exceptions.
