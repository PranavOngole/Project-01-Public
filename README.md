# An institutional research team. For one investor at a time.

The case for an individual investor making informed decisions has never been stronger. The information needed to make them has never been more abundant. And the gap between the data and the decision has never been wider.

Project-01 closes that gap.

A multi-agent research and execution platform that puts institutional-grade intelligence in front of the individual investor. Earnings transcripts, SEC filings, analyst revisions, insider moves, macro regime, options flow - read continuously, scored against a transparent framework, surfaced with the conviction language a professional fund would use, and executed against the rules the investor has set.

**Live:** [project-01-terminal.vercel.app](https://project-01-terminal.vercel.app)

---

## Why now

Three things, all simultaneously true in 2026 and not before:

**AI agents are good enough to do real work.** Not chatbots. Not autocomplete. Specialist agents that produce structured outputs against machine-readable contracts and refuse when their inputs are corrupted.

**The cost of running them continuously has collapsed.** What was a million-dollar research team is now a process that runs on every cron tick.

**Trust is a solvable problem.** Audit chains, source grounding, validated output contracts, and forensic test fixtures turn "the AI said so" into "verified before write."

The combination is not incremental. It is a step-change in who has access to research-grade conviction.

## What you see

**TERMINAL.** Pull up any ticker and get the complete picture on one screen.

Price action with annotated events. Options flow and unusual activity. Insider and institutional moves. A peer comparison that places the company in its cohort. A fundamental verdict with a distributional intrinsic-value band, an earnings-quality letter grade, and risk flags. Everything you would want before making a decision: already pulled, already structured, already ranked by what matters most.

**STRATEGY.** Define your investment criteria once. The system executes against them.

Set the universe to watch. Set entry and exit conditions in your own words. Set hard limits on position size, drawdown, and sector exposure. The system monitors every name in your universe, flags when your criteria are met, and executes trades within your guardrails - only when you have authorized it to.

This is not automated trading that replaces judgment. It is systematic execution of judgment you have already expressed. The investor defines the rules. The system enforces them with the consistency a human cannot maintain at 3 AM when a position moves.

**COCKPIT.** When the system is executing on your behalf, you see everything in real time.

Every position. Every decision and the reasoning behind it. Every risk limit and how close you are to it. A glass box, not a black box.

## What is in production today

Said in public language, without methodology:

- **Distributional intrinsic value.** Not a single price target. A range, with a labelled dominant-uncertainty driver and a four-tier conviction ladder that sizes by where the market sits in that range.
- **Earnings-quality letter grade.** A composite signal across cash conversion, accruals, manipulation flags, balance-sheet stress, and intangible bloat. Validated against an eight-name historical-fraud forensic fixture that fires on the right names and stays quiet on the wrong ones.
- **Two independent capital-exposure layers.** A recommendation-layer margin of safety. A separate trade-execution gate beneath that. Either can block a position before it is opened.
- **Source-grounded research with refusal semantics.** When the research stage cannot reach grounded sources, it refuses to fabricate output. The downstream conviction stage treats refusal as a hard signal rather than guessing.
- **Warehouse-first source of truth.** Bi-temporal contract on every fact: effective date, availability timestamp, supersession discipline, content hash, vendor provenance. Every output is replayable.
- **Operational discipline.** Single-domain release batches. Every shipped change carries a written rationale, the gates that passed, and the rollback path.
